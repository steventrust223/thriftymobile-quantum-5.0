üí∞ MAO & Offers ‚ñº

MAO = Maximum Allowable Offer based on buyback value minus target profit margin.

Offer Target = First offer to send (85% of MAO by default).

Risk adjustments and hot seller bonuses are applied automatically.

  • Priority in action recommendations
  • Tracked in LEADS_TRACKER
